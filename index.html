The "What If...?" Cascade Tracker

Speculative fiction maps alternate realities by tracing how one change ripples through a society. Let's build your story's foundation.

📋 Model Inquiry Journal Entry

Pillar: Innovation

Core "What If?": What if a massive solar flare permanently wiped out all digital payments overnight, forcing a hyper-cashless society to revert back to physical currency?

⚡ [IMMEDIATE IMPACT]: Total gridlock and immediate panic. People stranded at subway turnstiles because their phones can't scan QR codes to commute. Street food vendors can't sell breakfast, and grocery stores have to close their doors because they don't even have physical cash registers or paper money on the premises.
🏛️ [SOCIETAL RIPPLE]: Physical, tangible objects become the new bedrock of trust. The government has to rapidly print emergency paper currency, but a thriving underground barter system has already taken over, where people trade physical goods like rare pre-flare tech components, coffee, and physical books. "Unplugged" communities become highly sought-after, safe havens because they were never reliant on the grid to begin with.
👤 [HUMAN-SCALE STORY]: A 15-year-old student who has literally never touched paper money in their entire life has to navigate the black market. Their grandfather is sick, and the only person with the right medicine refuses to accept anything but a pristine, physical 1990s plastic holographic trading card. The student has to sneak into the abandoned, overgrown tech-district to scavenge for it.
